Features for Everyone

VConnect creates a seamless ecosystem, connecting users, cleaners, and administration

For Users

Report without Login: Allows users to send reports anonymously, without needing to create an account.

Instant Refill Requests: One-tap requests for soap, tissue, or sanitizer when missing.

Hassle-Free Experience: Scan the QR code and submit feedback within seconds.

For Cleaners

Real-Time Alerts: Receive instant notifications for any maintenance request.

Task Management: Mark tasks completed and manage cleaning schedules efficiently.

Digital Logs: Replace paper sheets with tamper-proof, digital records.

For Supervisors

Live Dashboard: Monitor the status of all washrooms across campus in real-time.

Performance Tracking: Track cleaner response times and resolution rates for feedback.

Inventory Management: Predict supply usage and prevent stockouts before they occur.
