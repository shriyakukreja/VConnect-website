How VConnect Works

A simple four-step process to ensure cleaner, safer campus facilities.

1. Scan

User finds the QR code in the washroom and scans it using their smartphone camera.

2. Report

A simple form opens. Select issues like no soap, dirty floor, or broken tap and submit.

3. Notify

The nearest cleaner receives an instant alert with the specific location details.

4. Resolve

The cleaner fixes the issue, updates the status, and supervisors track it on the dashboard.

Ready to improve your campus hygiene?

The system is designed to be plug and play. Simply scan the QR stickers and start monitoring.
