About CleanOps

CleanOps is a smart washroom monitoring and alert system. QR codes placed in each washroom link users and guests to a live report page where they can flag issues, rate cleanliness, and request immediate attention.

Reports flow directly to this supervisor dashboard, where tasks are auto-assigned to the staff member with the lowest current workload. Supervisors can override, reassign, or respond to queries in real time.
